# Chelsey M. Beese, Ph.D.

**Chelsey** is a marine scientist specializing in interdisciplinary research and ecosystem modeling. She is currently a postdoctoral scholar with the [Integrated Marine Fisheries Lab](https://cheryl-barnes.github.io/integrated-marine-fisheries.html) at [Oregon State University](https://marinesciences.oregonstate.edu/), working with Dr. Cheryl Barnes (OSU) and Dr. Jon Reum (NOAA) to advance NOAA's Alaska ([ACLIM](https://www.fisheries.noaa.gov/alaska/ecosystems/alaska-climate-integrated-modeling-project)) and Gulf of Alaska ([GOACLIM](https://www.fisheries.noaa.gov/alaska/ecosystems/gulf-alaska-climate-integrated-modeling-project)) Climate Integrated Modeling projects.
